IB Curriculum

At Manchester Global School, we are on the path to becoming an authorised IB World School, ensuring an internationally recognized education. Our inquiry-based IB Curriculum empowers students with a cohesive high school learning journey, fostering critical thinking, adaptability, and a global perspective.

CBSE Curriculum

Integral to our academic offerings, the CBSE Curriculum provides a robust foundation. Through interactive sessions and practical learning, students gain mastery and develop critical thinking abilities. This curriculum equips them with essential life skills, preparing them for real-world challenges.

Residential School with
5-day and 7-day
Boarding Options

Our residential boarding facilities provide students a place they can genuinely refer to as their second home. With separate buildings for boys and girls, air-conditioned rooms that are well-appointed, and floor in-charges/mentors who are specifically assigned to each floor, we guarantee a safe and caring atmosphere that supports student wellbeing.

We have 5-day and 7-day boarding options for a comprehensive experience that balances learning and homely comforts.
